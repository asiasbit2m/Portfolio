Employee Data Entry

The main form allows users to input employee details, such as name, position, and hours worked. It serves as the starting point for payroll calculations.

Payroll Calculation Engine

This section automatically calculates the employee's gross pay, tax deductions, and net pay based on their position and hours worked. It ensures accurate and timely payroll processing.

Payroll Summary Overview

The summary feature aggregates total gross pay, tax deductions, and net pay for all employees. It provides a quick snapshot of payroll data for efficient tracking and reporting.

Export Payroll Records

The export function enables users to save payroll data and summaries to a text file for easy storage. It simplifies record management and sharing with stakeholders.
